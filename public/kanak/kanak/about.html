A FEW WORDS OUR FIRM

Since the year 1997, Kanak Print-N-Pack, Ably led by our owner Mr.Mehta to outshine all competitors in all aspects. His astute judgmental skills and sharp business acumen is the reason behind our continuity and tremendous progress in the packaging industry. This has ensured that we have swiftly gained the pole position in our industry and has gained a well known name as one of the most reliable suppliers in the packaging industry and is engaged in manufacturing, supplying and exporting of a wide range of PVC and PP pouches, boxes and allied packaging material. Efficiently fulfilling the packaging requirements of diverse industries, our qualitative and exclusively designed products have earned us the trust of a wide customer base across the globe.Backed by our proficient experts, and state-of-the-art, technologically advanced equipment, we make use of latest screen and offset printing technology in order to provide customized printing services to make the package more appealing along with promoting the concerned product and company.Comprehending the importance of hygiene and safety factor in the packaging of certain goods, we make use of non-toxic PVC material and perform intensive research to develop innovative and enduring packaging products. Our ethical way of dealing and perseverance of transparency in communicating with our clients allow us to execute our business activities in a responsible manner with respect to social, environmental and economical aspects. We help not only beautify but ensure safety of your products so that they arrive in the customers hand in mint condition.
